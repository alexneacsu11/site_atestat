Welcome to the home page!

This is the home page. There you cand find usefull informations about this site.

This site has 7 pages that have different types of content , some of them having language switching tools from english to romanian and reverse , like this one.

The Gaming tab , will lead you to a collection of recordings from Kaly's Youtube Channel , where are either recorded videos , either streamed sessions.

The "Tools" tab, is similar to "Educatie"("Education") but instead of videos you will find images.

The "Educatie"(Education) tab , will show some tutorial like videos , that will teach you different things. The videos found here , are either recomandation , either from Kaly's secondary youtube account.

The "Vezi Live"(Watch Live) tab , will take you to a page where you can see the Live Stream of Kaly if it happens to be streaming in that time.

The "Despre"(About) tab , will show a page that provides informations about Kaly in romanian but also in english. The information you will find here contains a bit of creator's information but also kaly's information.

The "Contact-Discord" tab , is a form like page , that will let you send messages to Kaly . Also there you cand find information about how to contact him.
